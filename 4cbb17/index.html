We are the FTC team #17529 or 4cbb17. We are a group of 8 students and 3 mentors, our students range in age group from junior high to high school. We attend a 1A school, of about 285 students total in pre-k through 12th, in the small town of May, Texas. Our team is a rookie team in the eyes of the FTC competition since the 2019 season is our first FTC season, but before we started FTC we were in a smaller robotics competition called TCEA. We all have enjoyed this years competition and look forward to competing in more FTC competitions.
